Jobs

The Second City Summer program is built to work alongside your summer job or internship. Each participant is required to have a job or internship relatively close to the city center (i.e. not the suburbs). If you have questions or concerns about this, please let us know. The Second City Summer staff is happy to work with you to find an internship or job that works well for you and the program.

More information regarding specific job opportunities will be coming to this website soon!

Funding

In preparation for the program, each Second City Summer participant will be guided into the fundraising process to cover the cost of the program. We will work with you on this and give you help and direction. Our staff are well experienced in fundraising, and we look forward to walking with you as you invest in a life-changing summer!
